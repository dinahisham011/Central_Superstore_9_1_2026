# Central_Superstore_Dashboard_9_1_2026
This project presents an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard built using Power BI.
The dashboard provides insights into sales, profit, customer behavior, product performance, shipping methods, and geographic distribution to support data-driven business decisions.

The dataset represents transactional sales data across multiple product categories, customer segments, shipping modes, and U.S. locations.
# Business Objectives
The dashboard was designed to answer key business questions, including:
- How are sales and profit performing over time?
- Which product categories and sub-categories are profitable or loss-making?
- Which customers and products contribute most to revenue?
- How do shipping modes impact sales distribution?
- What is the sales contribution by customer segment and geography?
# Key KPIs
The dashboard highlights the following core KPIs:
- Total Sales: 501.24K
- Total Profit: 39.71K
- Total Orders: 1.18K
- Profit Margin: 7.92%

These KPIs provide a high-level executive view of overall business performance.

# Dashboard Features & Visuals
- Sales & Profit Trend Analysis
Monthly comparison of sales and profit to identify seasonal patterns and growth trends.
- Category & Sub-Category Performance
Comparative analysis of sales and profit across product categories and sub-categories, highlighting loss-making areas.
- Product Performance Analysis
  - Top 10 products by sales
  - Least profitable products to identify pricing or discount issues
- Customer Analysis
Top 10 customers by sales contribution.
- Segment Analysis
Sales distribution across Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments.
- Shipping Mode Analysis
Order distribution by shipping mode to understand operational preferences.
-Geographical Analysis
Sales distribution across U.S. cities using map visualization.
